Zeroth Robotics presents the **Humanoid Olympics**! We’re pioneering affordable, open-source humanoid robots and inviting you to be among the first to test and compete with our Zeroth-01 robots. Whether you're an enthusiast, developer, or hacker, bring your vision to life by joining us in Palo Alto for this one-of-a-kind experience!

## Event Overview

Three main challenges await you:
- **Boxing**: Develop teleoperation and vision capabilities.
- **Running**: Tackle locomotion challenges using classical methods and reinforcement learning.
- **Debate**: Bring speech-based AI to life.

## Logistics

- **Overnight Accommodations**
  - Available on a case-by-case basis, especially for non-local participants.
  - Please inform us if you plan to stay overnight; you're welcome to bring personal tents or bedding.

- **Parking**
  - Parking is limited, so please carpool if possible. Avoid blocking neighbors’ driveways.

## Prizes

- **1st Place**: **Bambu A1 Mini 3D Printer** – Fuel your next project with precision prototyping capabilities.
- **2nd Place**: **Zeroth-01 Humanoid Robot** – Continue your journey with your very own Zeroth-01!
- **3rd Place**: **High-Torque Actuator** – Ideal for pushing the limits in automation and prototyping.

## Equipment and Support

We’ll have a variety of tools available, including Jetsons, GPUs, 3D printers, and a fully-equipped electronics bench. For 3D printing needs, find Matt in the print garage for assistance.

## Guidelines

Projects will be judged on **technical execution** and ability to succeed at the challenges we've set forth. Only open-source projects are eligible for prizes.
